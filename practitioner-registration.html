Our Safeguarding Commitment — Together

Safeguarding at H.O.M.E Hub is something we hold together. By registering, I commit to reading and following the H.O.M.E Hub Safeguarding Policy, to working within the supervision arrangements agreed for my sessions, and to raising any concern — however small it feels — with the Designated Safeguarding Lead (Nichola Richardson) straight away. A copy of the policy is always available on site and on request from info@homehub-benfleet.co.uk.
